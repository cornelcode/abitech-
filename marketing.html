Marketing Department

Connecting Abitech’s vision to the world through strategy, creativity, and communication.

Brand Strategy and Positioning

The marketing team defines Abitech’s brand identity across all sectors — business, agriculture, and technology. They ensure consistent messaging that aligns with our values and long-term vision while maintaining a strong presence in global markets.

Digital Marketing and Online Presence

Through SEO, social media engagement, and content creation, the department drives Abitech’s digital footprint. They build campaigns that connect our innovations to audiences across platforms like LinkedIn, X, YouTube, and Instagram.

Market Research and Data Analytics

The marketing analytics team gathers insights on customer preferences, competitor trends, and market dynamics. These insights guide the company’s product launches, pricing models, and customer experience strategies.

Creative Design and Storytelling

Abitech’s creative marketing team crafts captivating visuals, videos, and interactive content. They translate technical innovations into relatable narratives that inspire investors, partners, and customers alike.

Barbara Thomas — Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

Barbara leads Abitech’s global marketing strategy, brand growth, and audience engagement initiatives. She works closely with the technology and business teams to align innovation with communication, ensuring that every project speaks Abitech’s values to the world.
