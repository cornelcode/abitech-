Security Department

Protecting Abitech’s data, people, and systems through innovation and vigilance.

Cybersecurity and Data Protection

The Security Department at Abitech safeguards digital assets through advanced encryption, firewalls, and AI-driven threat detection systems. Regular audits ensure that all sensitive data across our tech, business, and agriculture divisions remain secure from unauthorized access.

Risk Management and Compliance

Abitech employs a proactive risk management framework to identify and address vulnerabilities before they impact operations. We comply with international standards like ISO/IEC 27001 and GDPR to ensure our customers’ trust and regulatory alignment.

Physical and Network Security

Our infrastructure security extends beyond the digital realm. Physical access control, biometric systems, and 24/7 surveillance protect our facilities while maintaining smooth network performance and uptime reliability across continents.

Employee Awareness and Training

The department conducts regular training sessions and cybersecurity simulations to educate employees on safe digital practices. Every Abitech team member is part of our security ecosystem, ensuring resilience against phishing, malware, and social engineering threats.

Linda Martinez — Chief Security Officer (CSO)

Linda oversees all aspects of Abitech’s security strategy — from data protection and risk mitigation to regulatory compliance and crisis management. With over a decade of experience in enterprise cybersecurity, she ensures that innovation thrives safely within the Abitech ecosystem.
